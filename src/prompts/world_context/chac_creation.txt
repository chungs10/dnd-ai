# CHARACTER CREATION AND ADVANCEMENT
# For Elara's Player Character Management

RACE DESCRIPTIONS SPECIFIC TO EORZEA INCLUDE SEVERAL OPTIONS.

HYUR ARE THE HUMAN RACE OF EORZEA.
Hyur traits include being adaptable and ambitious.
Hyur abilities include plus one to two ability scores.
Hyur features include versatility and being skilled.
Hyur culture shows they are found everywhere with no single homeland.

ELEZEN ARE THE ELF LIKE RACE OF EORZEA.
Elezen traits include being graceful and long lived.
Elezen abilities include plus two dexterity and plus one intelligence or wisdom.
Elezen features include keen senses and trance meditation.
Elezen culture centers on ancient forests and scholarly pursuits.

LALAFELL ARE THE SMALL FOLK RACE OF EORZEA.
Lalafell traits include being cheerful and determined.
Lalafell abilities include plus two constitution and plus one charisma.
Lalafell features include small size and resilience.
Lalafell culture focuses on agriculture trade and family clans.

MIQO'TE ARE THE CAT LIKE RACE OF EORZEA.
Miqo'te traits include being agile and nocturnal.
Miqo'te abilities include plus two dexterity and plus one wisdom.
Miqo'te features include darkvision and climbing ability.
Miqo'te culture follows hunter gatherer traditions with moon worship.

ROEGADYN ARE THE GIANT LIKE RACE OF EORZEA.
Roegadyn traits include being strong and sea loving.
Roegadyn abilities include plus two strength and plus one constitution.
Roegadyn features include powerful build and swimming skill.
Roegadyn culture centers on seafaring and craftsmanship traditions.

CLASS ARCHETYPES INCLUDE SEVERAL DISTINCT TYPES.

FIGHTERS ARE MARTIAL CLASS CHARACTERS.
Fighter playstyle involves weapons mastery and frontline combat.
Fighter key abilities include strength or dexterity and constitution.
Fighter subclasses include champion and battle master variations.

MAGES ARE ARCANE CLASS CHARACTERS.
Mage playstyle involves spellcasting and battlefield control.
Mage key abilities include intelligence and dexterity or constitution.
Mage schools include evocation abjuration and divination magic.

ROGUES ARE SKILLED CLASS CHARACTERS.
Rogue playstyle involves stealth and precision attacks.
Rogue key abilities include dexterity and intelligence or charisma.
Rogue archetypes include thief assassin and arcane trickster.

CLERICS ARE DIVINE CLASS CHARACTERS.
Cleric playstyle involves healing and party support.
Cleric key abilities include wisdom and constitution or strength.
Cleric domains include life light and war divine aspects.

BACKGROUNDS PROVIDE CHARACTER HISTORY AND SKILLS.

SOLDIER BACKGROUND:
Soldier skills include athletics and intimidation.
Soldier equipment includes insignia rank and battle trophies.
Soldier feature includes military rank recognition.

SAGE BACKGROUND:
Sage skills include arcana and history knowledge.
Sage equipment includes research notes and ink supplies.
Sage feature includes library access privileges.

URCHIN BACKGROUND:
Urchin skills include sleight of hand and stealth.
Urchin equipment includes small knife and city map.
Urchin feature includes knowledge of city secrets.

GUILD ARTISAN BACKGROUND:
Guild artisan skills include insight and persuasion.
Guild artisan equipment includes tools and guild letter.
Guild artisan feature includes guild support network.

LEVEL UP MILESTONES MARK CHARACTER PROGRESSION.

LEVEL THREE brings subclass choice and signature abilities.
LEVEL FIVE brings extra attack or third level spells and proficiency bonus increase to three.
LEVEL EIGHT brings ability score improvement and new class features.
LEVEL ELEVEN brings major power spike and sixth level spells.
LEVEL FOURTEEN brings subclass capstone features.
LEVEL SEVENTEEN brings ninth level spells and legendary abilities.
LEVEL TWENTY brings true mastery and campaign ending power.

CHARACTER BONDS AND MOTIVATIONS DRIVE STORIES.

PERSONAL DRIVES INCLUDE VARIOUS MOTIVATIONS.
Revenge drive involves wrongs that must be righted.
Duty drive involves oaths or responsibilities.
Discovery drive involves knowledge or truth sought.
Protection drive involves people or places to guard.
Redemption drive involves past actions to atone for.
Power drive involves strength or authority desired.

WORLD CONNECTIONS TIE CHARACTERS TO EORZEA.
Family connections include living relatives and lineage.
Mentor connections include teachers guiding the character.
Rival connections include someone competing with the character.
Organization connections include guilds orders or companies.
Debt connections include owing something to someone.
Secret connections include hidden truths about the character.

ELARA PROVIDES CHARACTER CREATION PHILOSOPHY.
Characters should feel like they belong in Eorzea.
Character stories begin before the first dice roll.
Help players find where personal tales intersect with the world's epic.
