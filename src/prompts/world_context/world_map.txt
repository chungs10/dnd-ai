# EORZEA: COMPLETE GEOGRAPHY

## CONTINENTAL OVERVIEW
Aldenard: Main continent containing Black Shroud, Thanalan, Coerthas.
Vylbrand: Island chain home to Limsa Lominsa.
The Bounty: Southern islands.
The Cieldalaes: Eastern archipelago.

## DETAILED REGION BREAKDOWN:

### THE BLACK SHROUD (Elemental Territory)
- Geography: Ancient forest ruled by Elementals, sentient nature spirits.
- Settlements:
  New Gridania: Built after Calamity, blends with forest.
  Old Gridania: Original settlement, more traditional.
  The Hawthorne Hut: Conjurer's Guild headquarters.
- Dangers: Voidsent incursions, Ixali aggression, poachers.
- Resources: Lumber, herbs, crystal deposits.

### THANALAN (The Desert Realm)
- Geography: Arid deserts, rocky mesas, hidden oases.
- Settlements:
  Ul'dah: Built into cliffside, tiered districts.
  The Gold Saucer: Entertainment complex on Lake Gold.
  Little Ala Mhigo: Refugee settlement.
- Dangers: Bandits, Amalj'aa tribes, heat exhaustion.
- Resources: Minerals, jewels, trading crossroads.

### LA NOSCEA (The Sea Realm)
- Geography: Archipelago with cliffs, beaches, and sea caves.
- Settlements:
  Limsa Lominsa: City built on ships and bridges.
  Costa del Sol: Beach resort for wealthy.
  Wineport: Vineyards and wine production.
- Dangers: Pirates (some reformed), Sahagin, sea storms.
- Resources: Fish, salt, maritime trade.

### MOR DHONA (The Twinned Realm)
- Geography: Blighted by Calamity, crystals grow everywhere.
- Settlements:
  Revenant's Toll: Adventurer hub, constantly expanding.
  Saint Coinach's Find: Sharlayan research outpost.
- Significance: Aether-rich, near Carteneau Flats (Calamity ground zero).

### COERTHAS (The Frozen Highlands) [Currently inaccessible]
- Geography: Once temperate, now frozen after Calamity.
- Settlement: Ishgard: Fortress-city, isolated from Eorzean Alliance.
