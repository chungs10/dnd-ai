# DUNGEON MASTER GUIDELINES

## HOW TO RUN FFXIV AS D&D

**ADVENTURE STRUCTURE**:
1. **Hook**: Quest from Scions, Grand Company, or notice board.
2. **Exploration**: Travel through Eorzea, random encounters.
3. **Combat**: Primal fights, beast tribes, Garleans.
4. **Story**: Uncover Ascian plots, Echo visions.
5. **Reward**: Gil, gear, reputation, story progression.

**ENCOUNTER DIFFICULTY** (Based on player level):
- **Easy**: Beast tribe scouts, minor voidsent.
- **Medium**: Primal minions, Garlean soldiers.
- **Hard**: Primal avatars, Ascian puppets.
- **Deadly**: Full primals, Ascians themselves.

**NPC TEMPLATES**:
- **Scion Agent**: Level 5-10, Echo abilities, helpful.
- **Grand Company Soldier**: Level 3-6, loyal to city-state.
- **Beast Tribe Warrior**: Level 2-4, territorial.
- **Garlean Legionnaire**: Level 4-8, magitek armor.
- **Ascian**: Level 10+, shadowy, manipulative.

## QUEST TYPES

**MAIN SCENARIO QUEST** (MSQ):
- Epic storyline, levels 1-50.
- Involves primals, Ascians, saving Eorzea.
- Grants major rewards, unlocks content.

**SIDE QUESTS**:
- Local problems, fetch quests, minor threats.
- Rewards: Gil, experience, reputation.

**GRAND COMPANY QUESTS**:
- Military operations for chosen city-state.
- Unlocks ranks, special gear, mounts.

**DUNGEON QUESTS**:
- Multi-part adventures with boss fights.
- Requires party of 4-8 adventurers.
- Rewards: Dungeon-specific loot.

**RAID QUESTS** (High-end):
- Extreme primal fights, 8-player coordination.
- Requires optimized gear and strategy.

## WORLD REACTIONS

**REPUTATION SYSTEM** (Per city-state):
- **Hated**: Attacked on sight, no services.
- **Hostile**: Unfriendly, higher prices.
- **Neutral**: Standard interactions.
- **Friendly**: Discounts, assistance.
- **Revered**: Special quests, unique rewards.

**CONSEQUENCES**:
- Helping beast tribes → City-state disapproval.
- Defeating primals → Fame, Ascian attention.
- Choosing Grand Company → Permanent affiliation.
- Using Echo publicly → Scion recruitment.

## RANDOM TABLES

**TRAVEL ENCOUNTERS** (d20):
1-5: Peaceful journey
6-10: Beast tribe patrol
11-13: Garlean scout
14-16: Voidsent manifestation  
17-18: Merchant caravan
19: Echo vision (plot clue)
20: Primal sighting (danger!)

**LOOT DROPS** (After combat):
- Common: Gil pouch, potions, crafting materials.
- Uncommon: Magic weapon/armor, rare materials.
- Rare: Primal weapon replica, tomestones.
- Very Rare: Mount whistle, artifact piece.
