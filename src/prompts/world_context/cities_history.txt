# CITY-STATE HISTORIES

## GRIDANIA: THE FOREST CITY
Founding: Settled by Hyur and Elezen refugees after the Sixth Umbral flood.
Government: Theocratic rule by Seedseers (Padjal) who communicate with Elementals.
Military: Wood Wailers (defense), Gods' Quiver (archers), Twin Adders (Grand Company).
Key Events:
- Elementals initially hostile, only accepted settlers after Greenwrath appeasement.
- Maintains tense peace with Ixali beast tribe.
- Severely damaged during Calamity but rebuilt quickly.

## UL'DAH: THE DESERT JEWEL
Founding: Established by nomadic tribes around an oasis, grew into trading hub.
Government: Figurehead Sultana (Nanamo Ul Namo) with real power held by Monetarist Syndicate.
Military: Brass Blades (city guard), Immortal Flames (Grand Company).
Key Events:
- Constant power struggles between royalists and syndicate.
- Wealth inequality creates slums like Pearl Lane.
- Major refugee destination after Ala Mhigo's fall.

## LIMSA LOMINSA: THE MARITIME REPUBLIC
Founding: Former pirate fleet that settled, turned legitimate.
Government: Admiral (Merlwyb Bloefhiswyn) rules with pirate council.
Military: Yellowjackets (navy), Maelstrom (Grand Company).
Key Events:
- Founded by pirate captain Mistbeard who united warring crews.
- Reformed from piracy to legitimate trade and defense.
- Constant tension with Kobold and Sahagin beast tribes.

## COMMON THREADS:
- All three survived the Calamity through different means.
- All face primal threats from neighboring beast tribes.
- All part of the Eorzean Alliance but maintain independence.
- All suspicious of Garlean Empire's expansion.
