# EORZEA: A REALM REBORN - COMPLETE HISTORY

## THE ERAS OF EORZEA

**The Ancient Past (Pre-Umbral Eras):**
- **Allagan Empire** (~5000 years ago): Technological marvels, creation of Omega and Dalamud, fell due to hubris.
- **The War of the Magi**: Amdapor (white magic) vs Mhach (black magic) devastated the land.
- **The Sixth Umbral Era**: Great flood created modern geography, ended with Thaliak's intervention.

**The Age of City-States:**
- **Formation** (After Sixth Umbral): Survivors formed tribes that evolved into modern city-states.
- **Ala Mhigan Invasion** (20 years ago): King Theodoric conquered Ishgard, attempted to take Gridania.
- **Garlean Invasion** (15 years ago): Garlean Empire conquered Ala Mhigo, continues to threaten Eorzea.

**The Calamity Era:**
- **Year -1**: Dalamud begins descending, causing panic and cults.
- **Year 0 (The Calamity)**: 
  - Dalamud reveals itself as Allagan prison.
  - Bahamut breaks free, destroys Carteneau.
  - Louisoix attempts sealing, becomes primal Phoenix.
  - Aether sundered, land transformed, millions perish.
- **Year 1-4 (The Seventh Umbral Era)**:
  - "The Waking Sands" established by Minfilia.
  - Primals reappear: Ifrit (Amalj'aa), Titan (Kobolds), Garuda (Ixal).
  - Grand Companies form for mutual defense.
  - Echo-bearers emerge as "Warriors of Light".

**Current Era (Year 5 - A Realm Reborn):**
- Adventurers with Echo arrive in Eorzea.
- Scions actively combat primal threats.
- Garlean Empire builds Castrum Novum in Mor Dhona.
- Ascians manipulate events from shadows.

## KEY HISTORICAL FIGURES:
- **Louisoix Leveilleur**: Archon who sacrificed himself during the Calamity.
- **King Theodoric**: Mad king of Ala Mhigo who invaded neighbors.
- **Nael van Darnus**: White Raven who orchestrated the Calamity.
- **Archon Galuf Baldesion**: Founder of Students of Baldesion.
