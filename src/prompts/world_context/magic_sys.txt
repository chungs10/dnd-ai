# EORZEA MAGIC SYSTEM
# How Magic Works in the World

AETHER IS THE SOURCE OF ALL MAGIC.

AETHER COMES IN SEVERAL TYPES WITH DIFFERENT PROPERTIES.
Fire aether appears red and relates to destruction and passion.
Fire aether provides energy for magical effects.
Water aether appears blue and relates to healing and emotion.
Water aether controls flow and fluidity magic.
Earth aether appears yellow and relates to stability and protection.
Earth aether supports growth and defensive magic.
Wind aether appears green and relates to movement and freedom.
Wind aether connects to thought and mental magic.
Lightning aether appears purple and relates to speed and revelation.
Lightning aether produces shock and sudden effects.
Ice aether appears white and relates to preservation and stillness.
Ice aether provides clarity and preservation magic.

AETHER FLOWS THROUGH THE WORLD IN VARIOUS WAYS.
Ley lines are natural magic currents flowing through the land.
Aetherytes are crystal teleportation nodes that focus aether.
The aetherial sea is a collective unconscious and magic source.
Corruption occurs when aether becomes void-touched or aspected.

DIFFERENT TYPES OF MAGIC USERS EXIST IN EORZEA.

WHITE MAGES USE SPECIFIC MAGICAL SOURCES.
White mages draw power from elementals and nature itself.
White mages focus on healing and protective magic.
White mages face limitations in balancing with nature.
White mages face dangers like overhealing and elemental wrath.

BLACK MAGES USE DIFFERENT MAGICAL SOURCES.
Black mages draw power from internal aether and the void.
Black mages focus on destructive and elemental magic.
Black mages face limitations from aether depletion.
Black mages face dangers like self-damage and corruption.

ARCANISTS USE UNIQUE MAGICAL APPROACHES.
Arcanists use mathematics and geometry as their source.
Arcanists focus on summoning and creating constructs.
Arcanists face limitations from complex calculations.
Arcanists face dangers like control loss and faulty equations.

RED MAGES USE BALANCED MAGICAL APPROACHES.
Red mages draw from balanced aether sources.
Red mages focus on mixing swordplay with spellcasting.
Red mages face limitations in maintaining balance.
Red mages face dangers from imbalance consequences.

MAGIC HAS LAWS AND LIMITATIONS IN EORZEA.

CANTONS ARE LEGAL RESTRICTIONS ON MAGIC USE.
Magic is forbidden in most city centers.
Only licensed casters may practice magic openly.
Certain dangerous spells are completely banned.
Magical disturbances must be reported to authorities.

PHYSICAL LAWS GOVERN MAGICAL INTERACTIONS.
Conservation of aether means magic cannot be created from nothing.
Equivalent exchange requires equal sacrifice for magical effects.
Sympathetic connections allow magic through linked objects.
True names hold power over people and creatures.

DANGERS ACCOMPANY MAGICAL PRACTICE.
Aether sickness comes from magical overuse.
Primal temptation lures mages with promises of power.
Void corruption spreads through dark magic use.
Spell backlash punishes failed casting attempts.

MAGIC ITEM CREATION FOLLOWS SPECIFIC PROCESSES.

THE ENCHANTING PROCESS HAS SEVERAL STEPS.
First gather materials like crystals and magical reagents.
Second prepare the vessel by cleansing and attuning it.
Third inscribe runes and magical patterns.
Fourth channel aether into the prepared vessel.
Fifth perform the final binding ritual.

DIFFERENT TYPES OF MAGIC ITEMS EXIST.
Consumable items include potions and scrolls.
Charged items like wands and rods have limited uses.
Permanent items like weapons and armor provide constant effects.
Artifacts are unique powerful items often with sentience.

ELARA PROVIDES WISDOM ABOUT MAGIC USE.
When magic feels limitless it becomes meaningless.
Constraints create creativity in magical practice.
The cost makes magical power meaningful and balanced.
