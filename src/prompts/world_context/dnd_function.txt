# D and D MECHANICS IN EORZEA

CHARACTER CREATION RULES FOR EORZEA.

RACES IN FINAL FANTASY FOURTEEN WITH D and D STATS.
Hyur are similar to humans with plus one to all stats.
Hyur are adaptable and common throughout Eorzea.
Elezen are similar to elves with plus two dexterity and plus one intelligence.
Elezen have keen senses and enter trance states.
Lalafell are similar to halflings with plus two dexterity and plus one charisma.
Lalafell are lucky and brave in dangerous situations.
Miqo'te are similar to tabaxi with plus two dexterity and plus one charisma.
Miqo'te have natural climbing ability and increased speed.
Roegadyn are similar to goliaths with plus two strength and plus one constitution.
Roegadyn have stone's endurance for increased resilience.

CLASSES AS FINAL FANTASY FOURTEEN JOBS ADAPTED TO D and D.
Gladiator or paladin class serves as tank with holy magic.
Gladiator or paladin classes are oath bound to their codes.
Marauder or warrior class uses rage for heavy damage.
Marauder or warrior class serves as frontline tank.
Conjurer class combines cleric and druid aspects.
Conjurer class focuses on healing and nature magic.
Thaumaturge class serves as wizard with elemental destruction.
Thaumaturge class requires study and arcane knowledge.
Archer or bard class provides support and ranged combat.
Archer or bard class uses music and performance magic.
Pugilist or monk class fights unarmed with speed.
Pugilist or monk class requires discipline and training.
Rogue or ninja class uses stealth and precision.
Rogue or ninja class employs tricks and subterfuge.

ABILITY SCORES FOLLOW D and D STANDARD RULES.
Strength represents physical power and melee damage.
Dexterity represents agility stealth and ranged attacks.
Constitution represents health endurance and resilience.
Intelligence represents knowledge and thaumaturgy magic.
Wisdom represents perception intuition and nature magic.
Charisma represents persuasion performance and leadership.

COMBAT AND ADVENTURING MECHANICS.

INITIATIVE is determined by dexterity scores.
The highest dexterity determines who acts first in combat.

HIT POINTS represent character health.
Hit points are determined by class and constitution scores.

ARMOR CLASS is the defense value against attacks.
Higher armor class values are better for avoiding damage.

SAVING THROWS are ability checks to resist effects.
Saving throws use ability modifiers to resist spells and hazards.

REST MECHANICS ALLOW CHARACTER RECOVERY.
Short rest takes one hour and recovers some abilities.
Long rest takes eight hours and recovers all abilities and hit points.

SKILL CHECKS USE D TWENTY PLUS ABILITY MODIFIER PLUS PROFICIENCY.
Persuasion uses charisma to convince NPCs and negotiate.
Perception uses wisdom to notice hidden things and ambushes.
Stealth uses dexterity to hide sneak and move quietly.
Arcana uses intelligence for knowledge of magic and aether.
Survival uses wisdom to track forage and navigate terrain.

MAGIC SYSTEM COMBINES AETHER AND D and D RULES.

SPELL SLOTS represent limited magical energy per day.
Characters have specific numbers of spell slots by level.

SPELL LEVELS range from one to nine.
Higher spell levels are more powerful magical effects.

SPELL COMPONENTS ARE REQUIRED FOR CASTING.
Verbal components are spoken words in ancient languages.
Somatic components are hand gestures weaving aether.
Material components include aether crystals and magical foci.

SPELL SCHOOLS ARE ADAPTED FOR EORZEA.
Evocation school includes damage spells like fire blizzard and thunder.
Conjuration school includes summoning and healing spells like cure and raise.
Abjuration school includes protection spells and wards like protect and shell.
Divination school includes information and echo related magic.
Transmutation school includes changing matter and shapeshifting magic.

LOOT AND ECONOMY SYSTEMS.

CURRENCY IN EORZEA INCLUDES SEVERAL TYPES.
Gil is the standard currency throughout the realm.
Copper gil equals one gold piece value.
Silver gil equals ten gold pieces value.
Gold gil equals one hundred gold pieces value.
Allagan tomestones are ancient currency for rare items.

Common treasure includes minor magic items.
Uncommon treasure includes plus one weapons.
Uncommon treasure includes useful scrolls.
Uncommon treasure includes aetheryte shards.
Rare treasure includes artifact weapons.
Rare treasure includes powerful relics.
Rare treasure includes primal drops.
Legendary treasure includes zodiac weapons.
Legendary treasure includes ultimate raid drops.

MAGIC ITEMS HAVE SPECIFIC EFFECTS.
