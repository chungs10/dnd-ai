# D&D MECHANICS IN EORZEA

## CHARACTER CREATION

**RACES** (FFXIV races with D&D stats):
- **Hyur** (Human): +1 to all stats, adaptable, common.
- **Elezen** (Elf): +2 DEX, +1 INT, keen senses, trance.
- **Lalafell** (Halfling): +2 DEX, +1 CHA, lucky, brave.
- **Miqo'te** (Tabaxi): +2 DEX, +1 CHA, climbing, speed.
- **Roegadyn** (Goliath): +2 STR, +1 CON, stone's endurance.

**CLASSES** (FFXIV jobs as D&D classes):
- **Gladiator/Paladin** (Paladin): Tank, holy magic, oath-bound.
- **Marauder/Warrior** (Barbarian): Rage, heavy damage, tank.
- **Conjurer** (Cleric/Druid): Healing, nature magic.
- **Thaumaturge** (Wizard): Elemental destruction, study.
- **Archer/Bard** (Bard/Ranger): Support, ranged, music.
- **Pugilist/Monk** (Monk): Unarmed, speed, discipline.
- **Rogue/Ninja** (Rogue): Stealth, precision, tricks.

**ABILITY SCORES** (D&D standard):
- STR (Strength): Physical power, melee damage.
- DEX (Dexterity): Agility, stealth, ranged attacks.
- CON (Constitution): Health, endurance, resilience.
- INT (Intelligence): Knowledge, magic (thaumaturgy).
- WIS (Wisdom): Perception, intuition, nature magic.
- CHA (Charisma): Persuasion, performance, leadership.

## COMBAT & ADVENTURING

**INITIATIVE**: Dexterity determines who acts first in combat.
**HIT POINTS**: Health, determined by class and Constitution.
**ARMOR CLASS**: Defense value, higher is better.
**SAVING THROWS**: Ability checks to resist effects.

**REST MECHANICS**:
- **Short Rest**: 1 hour, recover some abilities.
- **Long Rest**: 8 hours, recover all abilities and HP.

**SKILL CHECKS** (d20 + ability modifier + proficiency):
- **Persuasion** (CHA): Convince NPCs, negotiate.
- **Perception** (WIS): Notice hidden things, ambushes.
- **Stealth** (DEX): Hide, sneak, move quietly.
- **Arcana** (INT): Knowledge of magic, aether.
- **Survival** (WIS): Track, forage, navigate.

## MAGIC SYSTEM (AETHER + D&D)

**SPELL SLOTS**: Limited magical energy per day.
**SPELL LEVELS**: 1-9, higher = more powerful.
**SPELL COMPONENTS**:
- **Verbal** (V): Spoken words in ancient languages.
- **Somatic** (S): Hand gestures, weaving aether.
- **Material** (M): Aether crystals, foci.

**SPELL SCHOOLS** (Adapted):
- **Evocation**: Damage spells (Fire, Blizzard, Thunder).
- **Conjuration**: Summoning, healing (Cure, Raise).
- **Abjuration**: Protection, wards (Protect, Shell).
- **Divination**: Information, Echo-related magic.
- **Transmutation**: Changing matter, shapeshifting.

## LOOT & ECONOMY

**CURRENCY**:
- **Gil**: Standard currency (copper = 1gp, silver = 10gp, gold = 100gp).
- **Allagan Tomestones**: Ancient currency for rare items.

**TREASURE**:
- **Common**: Potions, basic weapons, minor magic items.
- **Uncommon**: +1 weapons, useful scrolls, aetheryte shards.
- **Rare**: Artifact weapons, powerful relics, primal drops.
- **Legendary**: Zodiac weapons, ultimate raid drops.

**MAGIC ITEMS**:
- **Potion of Healing**: Restores 2d4+2 HP.
- **Aetheryte Earring**: Bonus to teleportation magic.
- **Chocobo Whistle**: Summons mount once per day.
- **Primal Essence**: Grants resistance to element.
