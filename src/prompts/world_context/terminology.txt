# EORZEA: ESSENTIAL TERMINOLOGY

## CORE CONCEPTS

**AETHER**: 
- The life force energy that flows through all living things and the planet itself.
- Source of all magic, measured in "aetherytes" for teleportation.
- Can be corrupted (tainted aether) or drained (by primals).

**THE ECHO**:
- A rare ability that manifests in some individuals after the Calamity.
- Allows perception of past events (visions) and understanding of all languages.
- Makes bearers immune to primal tempering.
- Sometimes called "the Blessing of Light."

**THE CALAMITY**:
- The Seventh Umbral Calamity, occurring 5 years ago.
- Bahamut's release from the moon Dalamud, devastating Eorzea.
- Caused massive geological changes, aether disruption, and societal collapse.

**WARRIORS OF LIGHT**:
- Adventurers blessed with the Echo who actively defend Eorzea.
- Recognized by their ability to combat primals without being tempered.
- Often work with the Scions of the Seventh Dawn.

## MAGICAL & SUPERNATURAL

**PRIMALS** (EIKONS):
- God-like beings summoned by beast tribes using crystals and prayer.
- Drain region's aether, threatening environmental collapse.
- Can "temper" followers into mindless worshipers.
- Examples: Ifrit (Amalj'aa), Titan (Kobolds), Garuda (Ixal).

**TEMPERING**:
- Process where primals transform followers into fanatical worshipers.
- Irreversible mental and physical alteration.
- Echo-bearers are immune to tempering.

**ELEMENTALS**:
- Nature spirits of the Black Shroud that control the forest.
- Can unleash "Greenwrath" (natural disasters) if angered.
- Communicate through Padjal (Seedseers).

**VOIDSENT**:
- Demonic creatures from the Void (13th reflection).
- Summoned by voidmages, threaten all living things.
- Examples: Ahriman, Succubus, Void Reaper.

## POLITICAL & MILITARY

**GRAND COMPANIES**:
- Military organizations of each city-state:
  *Order of the Twin Adders* (Gridania)
  *Immortal Flames* (Ul'dah)  
  *Maelstrom* (Limsa Lominsa)
- Recruit adventurers, defend territories, combat threats.

**SCIONS OF THE SEVENTH DAWN**:
- Secretive organization combating primal threats.
- Led by Minfilia, headquarters at The Waking Sands.
- Employs Echo-bearers and scholars.

**EORZEAN ALLIANCE**:
- Mutual defense pact between Gridania, Ul'dah, and Limsa Lominsa.
- Formed after the Calamity for collective security.
- Coordinates Grand Company efforts.

**GARLEAN EMPIRE**:
- Expansionist empire from the north (Ilsabard).
- Conquered Ala Mhigo 15 years ago.
- Uses magitek (magic + technology), opposes primals and aether use.

## RACES & PEOPLES

**BEAST TRIBES**:
- Non-human races with tribal societies:
  *Amalj'aa* (Thanalan) - Summon Ifrit
  *Kobolds* (La Noscea) - Summon Titan
  *Ixal* (Black Shroud) - Summon Garuda
  *Sahagin* (seas) - Summon Leviathan
  *Sylphs* (Black Shroud) - Summon Ramuh
- Often summon primals out of desperation or tradition.

**PLAYABLE RACES**:
- **Hyur**: Most common, adaptable humans.
- **Elezen**: Tall, long-eared, traditional forest dwellers.
- **Lalafell**: Small, agrarian people from southern islands.
- **Miqo'te**: Cat-like hunters with tribal heritage.
- **Roegadyn**: Large, seafaring people from northern seas.

## LOCATIONS & TRAVEL

**AETHERYTES**:
- Magical teleportation crystals in settlements.
- Allow instant travel for attuned individuals.
- Powered by local aether.

**CHOCOBOS**:
- Large, rideable birds used for transportation.
- Can be trained for combat (chocobo companions).
- Various colors indicate breeding and traits.

**AIRSHIPS**:
- Magitek flying vessels for inter-city travel.
- Require Grand Company affiliation to use.
- Operated by Garlond Ironworks.
