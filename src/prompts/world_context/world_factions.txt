# EORZEA: FACTIONS & ORGANIZATIONS

## MAJOR FACTIONS

**SCIONS OF THE SEVENTH DAWN**
- **Purpose**: Combat primal threats, research the Echo.
- **Leader**: Minfilia
- **Headquarters**: The Waking Sands (Vesper Bay, Thanalan)
- **Key Members**: Thancred, Y'shtola, Urianger, Yda, Papalymo
- **Allies**: All three city-states, Students of Baldesion

**GRAND COMPANIES**
- **Order of the Twin Adders** (Gridania):
  *Commander*: Serpent Marshal Swethryk Brookstone
  *Focus*: Forest defense, nature preservation
  
- **Immortal Flames** (Ul'dah):
  *Commander*: General Raubahn Aldynn  
  *Focus*: Desert security, refugee protection
  
- **Maelstrom** (Limsa Lominsa):
  *Commander*: Admiral Merlwyb Bloefhiswyn
  *Focus*: Naval dominance, anti-piracy

## BEAST TRIBES

**AMALJ'AA** (Thanalan)
- **Home**: Burning Wall, eastern Thanalan
- **Primal**: Ifrit, Lord of the Inferno
- **Culture**: Warrior society, worship fire
- **Conflict**: Clash with Ul'dah over territory

**KOBOLDS** (La Noscea)  
- **Home**: O'Ghomoro mines, western La Noscea
- **Primal**: Titan, the Lord of Crags
- **Culture**: Mining, tunnel-dwelling
- **Conflict**: Territory disputes with Limsa Lominsa

**IXAL** (Black Shroud)
- **Home**: Natalan, north Shroud
- **Primal**: Garuda, the Lady of the Vortex  
- **Culture**: Aerial, nest-building
- **Conflict**: Wood poaching in Shroud

## SCHOLARLY GROUPS

**THE STUDENTS OF BALDESION**
- **Founder**: Galuf Baldesion (deceased)
- **Focus**: Researching the Echo, ancient civilizations
- **Headquarters**: Unknown
- **Relation**: Allied with Scions

**THE ARCHONS**
- **Definition**: Scholars granted title by Sharlayan Studium
- **Examples**: Louisoix, Y'shtola, Urianger
- **Expertise**: Aetheric research, historical study

## SHADOW ORGANIZATIONS

**THE ASICIANS**
- **Nature**: Immortal, shadowy manipulators
- **Goal**: Rejoin the reflections, restore ancient world
- **Methods**: Manipulate conflicts, empower primals
- **Known Members**: Lahabrea, Elidibus, Nabriales

**THE MONETARIST SYNDICATE**
- **Location**: Ul'dah
- **Power**: True rulers behind the Sultana
- **Members**: Wealthy merchant lords
- **Goal**: Profit above all, control economy
